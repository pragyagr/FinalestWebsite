Admission on the basis of Sprts Excellence in Extra-Curricular Activities



Admission to various courses in the academic year 2015-16 on the basis of extracurricular activity (ECA) will be strictly as per University of Delhi guidelines. Under these guidelines ECA committee has been constituted which will be recommending students for admission on the basis of extracurricular activity.

Students seeking admission under the ECA category should fill the ECA form obtainable from the college. ECA entitles students to a concession of 5% in the cut- off percentage of the second list of the college in the relevant course of study. There are a limited number of seats available under this category and only those with outstanding achievement should apply.

Students are admitted on the basis of trials conducted by the college. Each student is compulsorily required to appear for trials and can appear for trial for maximum two activities. Students are required to bring their own equipment, materials, sound accompaniments, costumes, musical instruments, etc. as may be required for the trials. Academic performance will carry weightage in the selection process Admissions under the ECA category take place usually after the second cut-off list of the general admissions. The Complete schedule for the trials will be notified on the college website (www.dr.du.ac.in) and notice board.

Students must have documentary evidence of following over the past three years:

1) Participation in the said activity/event at inter- school, inter-district, state, national level etc.

2) Formal training in the said field from recognized bodies.

3) Proof of performances in public domain.

      Candidates appearing for the trials are requested to bring all original certificates/awards etc testifying the above.

      Following are the activities on the basis of which a candidate can seek admission under the ECA category :

1) Dramatics ( English/Hindi)

2) Fine Arts

3) Music (Vocal/Instrumental )

4) Debating (English/Hindi)

5) Dance (Classical/Western )

6) Creative writing (English/ Hindi)

Candidates are required to attach attested copies of the following, while filling in the form under the ECA category (original certificates for the same should be presented at the time of trials) :

1) Documents in support of achievements in ECA, on the basis of which admission is sought ( for last 3 years only ).

2) A letter of recommendation from the head/ principal/ officiating authority of the institution last attended, giving details of the activities in which the candidate has participated. This letter is not mandatory but desirable.

3) Statement of Purpose written by the candidate stating how can she contribute to the cultural life of the college and reasons for her choosing our college.

4) It is desirable if Portfolios are also attached especially for those applying under the category of fine arts.

5) Those applying under the category of performing arts are required to submit a recording of their performances in a CD.

6) Mark sheet on the basis of which the admission is sought.

7) Provisional/ pass certificate of last exam passed.

8) Conduct / character certificate from the institution last attended.

9) School Examination certificate (class x certificate), stating date of birth.

10) Transfer/ Migration certificate, if any.

11) Caste certificate (reserved categories - SC/ ST/ OBC)

Note: The college reserves the right not to make admissions under the category if no candidate is found suitable. The decision of the college regarding selections will be final and binding. No queries towards the same will be entertained. Students admitted under the ECA categories
