Note:

  1. The DD should be made in the favour of Principal, Daulat Ram College and Payable at Delhi.
  2. Details for Internet Banking: IFSC: IOBA0001734
    Name: Daulat Ram College
    Account No.: 173401000010000
    Branch: University Branch
    Bank: Indian Overseas Bank
    Name of the Student, Application Form No., Roll No. and course of study should be given in Remark.
  3. Foreign students will have to pay an amount in Indian Rupees equivalent of US 100 per year in addition to above.
  4. Student from other Universities will have to pay Rs.50/- as enrolment fee in addition to the above mentioned charges in M.A. Courses
  5. The Students are advised to write their name, year, Roll No. and course on the reverse of the DD and keep a copy for record.
  6. Duplicate Identity card fee is Rs. 50.
  7. In case of withdrawal, the fee will be refunded after deductions according to the University rule through RTGS/NEFT. Student should have her own account in any bank.
